Notice: All these mods were intended for smash. Therefore we cannot guarantee that they (or the controller if it is a serious functional mod) will work with other games.

Snapback Fix

Accidentally doing reverse neutral B's after flicking your thumbstick left or right? Accidental B-reverse? This mod solves this problem by adding a capacitor to the stickbox.

Controller will slightly drift to the right when plugged in at first. Resetting it with X+Y+Start, will fix it.*

*Must be done every time the controller is plugged in.

Button Mods

Button Remapping

Remap a button to another button! It’s genius! Good for games that do not have button remapping options. (I’m looking at you melee)
Notice: Light shield can be remapped to a button but it will not be tournament legal for OK!

Button Height Decreasing

A certain button too high? Hurts or awkward to press? Hard to slide to/from it? Get your button shorted from the bottom to a desired height.

Button Press Softening

The buttons rest on a capacitive pad, it’s the pad that offers the click and gives resistance. We can modify the pad to decrease the amount of force it takes to press a button.

Trigger Mods

Tactile Trigger Mod

Replaces the analog sliders and digital press with a tactile switch making the the triggers similar to 3DS triggers. Presses are quick and precise, mashing is much easier on them. Also helps reduce strain for people who “claw” the triggers.


Half Trigger Mod

Keeps the original trigger mechanism, but raises the point at which the digital press happens. Makes for a quick full press to power shield instantly. Can be used with the original capacitive pad, or the pad can be replaced with a tactile switch.




Spring Removal

Removes the spring out of the trigger mechanism. Results in your triggers always being depressed and allows for instant power shielding in melee and brawl. You will no longer be able to light shield in melee!
Free with a more expensive mod!

Disable Analog Slider

Trigger mechanism is kept as is, but the analog slider is disabled. Results in your triggers feeling the same, but will only activate when you hit the digital press at the bottom. You will no longer be able to light shield in melee!

Trigger Click Softening

Trigger Click Softening
The trigger buttons work on the same concept as the ABXY buttons. This mod will decrease the amount of effort required to press the button at the bottom of the triggers. Good for new Sm4sh controllers!

Rumble Pak Mods

Rumble Pak Removal

Disables and removes rumble motor for a lighter controller.
Free with a more expensive mod!

Rumble Pak Disconnection

Rumble motor is disabled but left within the controller to retain weight.
Free with a more expensive mod!

Tap Jump Switch

Melee does not have a turn tap jump off option like brawl and smash 4 have. This mod adds a switch to the back of your controller that can turn tap jump on and off.

Flat Buttons

This mod uses your original buttons. They get filed down to become smooth and letter-less (No ABXY letters). They feel very different from the gloss coat originally on the buttons, but sliding inputs on them is easier and feels "better" IMO. The feel of these buttons really boils down to preference.
