FAQ

Where are you in OK?
Tulsa. Come to TSO guys, Kaze will love you.

Whats the warranty like?
Limited warranty is 30 days. Warranty covers DOA, and mod failure. It does not cover damage done by the player. If you dropped your controller or spiked it and X or Y stopped working, sorry but that’s not covered.

Will my controller work with other games with X mod?
There are notices at the bottom of each mod description listing what it will work with after the mod has been done. For example: If you get the tactile triggers, games like MP, crash, and brawl will not read the trigger inputs, but smash4 and melee would be fine.

Why are your prices so pricey?
Startups are pricey.

Why does the turnaround time take so long?
Doing most of these mods takes time, this isn’t my main job, and there’s probably a queue already.
